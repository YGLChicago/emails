Chi Hack Night is a weekly hack-a-thon where govies, technologists, journalists, and concerned citizens come together to build a 21st century government that works. YGL is proud to partner with Chi Hack Night to bring Bureaucracy Busters to Chicago's largest, weekly civic hack night. Contribute to the successful launch of this exciting collaboration.

Providing transparent and accessible platforms which deliver services to our communities is only possible if civil servants help build solutions which are complete, compatible, and compliant.

Join your public service colleagues at Chi Hack Night! Whether you are participating in YGL's Bureaucracy Busters, a consultancy of public servants who help technologists think through navigating complexities of government bureaucracy and agency politics, or you want to join another civic tech project to affect change in your community, there is a place for you!

Oh, and did we mention? Empanadas and refreshments are served to attendees so you'll have plenty of energy to fuel your creativity.
